Design philosophy

I think the good design is intuitive, helpful and beautiful.

I think the design is not just about doing things right but also about do the right things. A successful design is the one that matches the user's demand and keeps the product/team/company growing.

I think the designer is the bridge between the manager and the executor(programmer or PR). So knowing the source and limitation of both parts is necessary for making an executable design.
